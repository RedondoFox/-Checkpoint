Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our FAQ section for quick answers to common questions.

  • Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page.
  • Q: How can I customize my planets?
    A: Go to your profile settings and select "Customize your planets."
  • Q: Where can I find my achievements?
    A: Your achievements are listed under the "Achievements" section in your profile.
  • Q: How do I contact support?
    A: Use the "Contact Us" section below to reach out to us.
  • Q: Can I delete my account?
    A: Yes, you can delete your account in the "Settings" section of your profile.
