Community Guidelines

Welcome to Lunar Leap, where productivity meets imagination! Our community is built on the shared goal of turning everyday tasks into an inspiring journey—one leap at a time. Whether you're building your dream planet or simply checking off groceries, every action helps you grow.

To keep our space thriving, safe, and fun for everyone, please follow these community guidelines: 1.Respect All AstronautsTreat others as fellow explorers. Be kind, constructive, and supportive—everyone’s goals are different, and that’s what makes our galaxy beautiful. 2.Celebrate Progress Cheer on others when they complete a task or grow their planet! Encourage, don’t compete. We're not racing to the moon; we're building worlds, one step at a time. 3. Keep It Relevant Discussions, comments, and shared lists should relate to productivity, goal-setting, and personal development. Off-topic chatter can orbit elsewhere. 4. No Dark Matter (aka Harmful Behavior) We have zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, spam, or any content that disrupts the peaceful orbit of this community. Report issues—our team is here to help. 5. Keep It Real—and Cosmic While Lunar Leap is fun and whimsical, please don’t share misleading, false, or harmful advice. Be honest in your to-do journeys and share authentically. 6. Privacy is Gravity Respect the privacy of others. Don’t share anyone’s personal info without consent. The galaxy is big enough for everyone’s boundaries. 7. Build Your Planet, Not Someone Else’s Do not copy or plagiarize other users' custom to-do ideas or content. Be inspired, but make your own leap. Final Star Thought Lunar Leap is more than an app—it’s a launchpad for your best self. Together, we’re creating not just task lists, but tiny universes full of possibility. Keep leaping, keep dreaming, and keep building the world you want—one completed task at a time.
